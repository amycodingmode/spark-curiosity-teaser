Scenes

What is Scenes?

One canvas. Every element.
Total control.

Scenes is a new capability inside Forma that turns multiple AI-generated elements into one perfectly composed image. One canvas where every element — products, figures, backdrops, props — lives together, lit together, and behaves together. Not a generator. Not an editor. The place where your generated work becomes finished.

Built for designers who:

▪ Compose with multiple AI-generated elements and need them to feel like
one photograph with details and depth
▪ Care about light direction, shadow physics, and edge fidelity at the pixel level
▪ Want to refine one part of an image without rebuilding the rest
▪ Demand precision the existing AI workflow has never offered

Process

How Scenes Work

Step 1

Bring your elements in.

Drop any AI-generated element onto the Scenes canvas. A product. A figure. A backdrop. A prop. Mix elements from different generations, different prompts, different moments of inspiration — Scenes accepts them all onto one shared workspace.

Step 2

Place them in space.

Position each element exactly where it belongs. Scale it. Rotate it. Set its depth. Scenes understands three-dimensional space, so a vase doesn't just sit on a table — it sits behind the candle and in front of the window. Every element knows where it stands.

Step 3

Layer with true depth.

Stack elements with z-depth awareness. Scenes calculates how each layer interacts with the others — what occludes what, what reflects what, what casts onto what — so your composition behaves like a real photographed space, not a flattened collage.

Step 4

Adjust each element on its own.

Every layer keeps its own controls. Brighten the subject without touching the background. Soften the depth-of-field on the foreground while keeping the distance crisp. Warm the key light on one object while leaving another in cool shadow. Independent control, indefinitely editable.

Step 5

Refine without losing the whole.

This is what changes everything. Regenerate a single element — swap the chair, restyle the jacket, replace the sky — and Scenes preserves the rest of your composition exactly as you composed it. No more starting over. No more cascading edits. The part changes; the whole remains.
